Welcome to Augustana

We are a nationally-recognized and top-ranked liberal arts school located in Sioux Falls South Dakota situated between the rugged beauty of the Black Hills and the urban skylines of Minneapolis. For those who aspire for more than the ordinary, we invite you to Go Viking!
More About Augustana

Explore the Community

We call it “the ultimate college town” because it is! Our location in Sioux Falls, a city of more than 160,000 known for its robust economy, thriving business environment and vibrant arts scene, leads to choice internship, clinical, research, student teaching and service experiences for our students. On campus, you'll explore your interests and find your passion from more than 50 majors and pre-professional specializations within a 4-1-4 academic calendar while building connections and friendships with nearly 1,800 students from 30 states and 44 countries around the globe.
